Dr. Ashutosh Richhariya

Director

Dr. Ashutosh Richhariya working as the scientist and head of the Engineering Translational Research group at L V Prasad Eye Institute (LVPEI). Having been diagnosed with a complicated eye disease, he wanted to ensure no one else undergoes the same trouble of surgical complications. Hence, Ashutosh using engineering knowledge wanted to find methods of improving it.

Pursued his PhD on the optical quality of the cornea under stress and hypothesized that, the topography and the optical quality of the cornea is due to the stresses in the suture, mechanical anisotropy in the cornea and the stress induced birefringence. Only person in state to receive the Nehru-Fulbright Scholarship, and was placed at The Ohio State University and The University of Rochester for further research.

Area of expertise is Corneal Biomechanics, Finite Element Modeling and Optical Instrumentation. His work at LVP focuses on use of engineering knowledge in medicine and health, and has helped in setting up an ecosystem of core research development for medical devices in India.
